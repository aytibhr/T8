TERMS & CONDITIONS

Please read the rules of the game carefully before plugging in.

1. General Rules

By entering Terminal 8 Gaming Cafe, you agree to abide by the following rules:

  • No outside food or drinks are allowed on the premises.
  • Treat all gaming equipment (consoles, controllers, steering wheels, pool cues) with respect. Any deliberate damage will be charged to the user.
  • Keep noise levels reasonable. Friendly banter is welcome, but excessive shouting or abusive language will not be tolerated.
  • Management reserves the right to refuse service to anyone violating the rules.

2. Booking & Cancellation

  • Bookings can be made online or on-site. Priority seating is given to pre-booked slots and members according to their tier.
  • Please arrive 10 minutes before your booked slot. If you are late by more than 15 minutes without notifying us, your slot may be given to waiting players.
  • Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance.

3. Memberships & T8 Coins

Terminal 8 offers tiered monthly memberships (Silver, Gold, Platinum). Each membership runs for 30 days from the date of activation. Memberships provide you with T8 Coins which can be used to book different stations flexibly.

3.1 T8 Coins System & Station Split

Instead of fixed hours for a single station, memberships grant you T8 Coins. You can mix and match your playtime across different stations:

  • PS5 Individual Station: 2 T8 Coins / Hour
  • Car Simulator: 4 T8 Coins / Hour
  • Group Lounge: 6 T8 Coins / Hour
  • Billiards: 6 T8 Coins / Hour

Based on this, here is how you can use your membership:

  • Silver (30 T8 Coins): Up to 15 hours of Individual Gaming OR 5 hours of Lounge OR ~7 hours of Car Simulator OR 5 hours of Billiards, or any combination.
  • Gold (80 T8 Coins): Up to 40 hours of Individual Gaming OR ~13 hours of Lounge OR 20 hours of Car Simulator OR ~13 hours of Billiards, or any combination.
  • Platinum (200 T8 Coins): Up to 100 hours of Individual Gaming OR ~33 hours of Lounge OR 50 hours of Car Simulator OR ~33 hours of Billiards, or any combination.

3.2 T8 Coins Conditions of Use

  • Non-Transferable: Memberships and T8 Coins are strictly non-transferable and can only be used by the registered member. Sharing your account will result in immediate termination without a refund.
  • Rollover: Unused T8 Coins roll over to the next month only if the membership is renewed before expiration. Otherwise, they expire.
  • Unlimited Perks: Platinum unlimited off-peak gaming does not deduct from your T8 Coins balance.

3.3 Priority Booking Rules

  • Silver (Basic Priority): Can book slots up to 3 days in advance.
  • Gold (Priority Booking): Can book slots up to 7 days in advance. Dedicated stations are held for Gold members until 2 hours prior to the slot.
  • Platinum (VIP Priority): Immediate queue jumping for walk-ins if a non-reserved station is available. Can book slots up to 14 days in advance and reserve specific preferred stations.

3.4 Additional Perks

  • Guest Passes: Platinum members receive 2 free guest passes per month. Each pass allows one friend to play alongside you for up to 2 hours for free.
  • Tournament Entries: Gold members get 1 free entry per month. Platinum members get free entry to all standard tournaments.

3.5 Discounts

Snack discounts (5%, 10%, 20%) apply to all items in the Cafe Corner for the member only. It cannot be applied to purchases made for large groups.

4. Group Lounge & Billiards

  • The Group Lounge is designed for 4-6 players. The hourly rate is fixed regardless of whether 4, 5, or 6 people are playing.
  • Billiards room is private. Misuse of the slate table (e.g., sitting on the table, spilling drinks, tearing the cloth) will result in immediate termination of the session and a damage fee.

5. Liability

Terminal 8 Gaming Cafe is not responsible for any lost, stolen, or damaged personal belongings. Please keep an eye on your valuables.
